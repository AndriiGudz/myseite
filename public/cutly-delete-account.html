Cutly – Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your Cutly account and personal data

You can delete your Cutly account in one of the following ways:

Option 1: Inside the app

Open the Cutly app → Settings → Delete account.

Your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2: Via email request

Send an email to: andriigudz.de@gmail.com

Subject: Account deletion request

Please include:

  • The email address used for registration
  • A short confirmation that you want to delete your account

What data will be deleted:

  • User account (email, authentication data)
  • Personal preferences
  • Saved meals and settings

Data retention:

  • All personal data is deleted immediately or within 30 days.
  • No data is kept after deletion, except where legally required.
