Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-25
Last updated: 2026-01-19

This Privacy Policy describes how the Cutly mobile application (the “App”, “we”, “our service”) collects, uses, and protects users’ personal data.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. What data we collect

1.1 Data you provide

  • email address
  • name or display name (if provided)
  • nutrition settings, preferences, and restrictions
  • user profile data

1.2 Data created while using the app

  • generated menus and recipes
  • shopping lists
  • feature usage history

1.3 Technical and analytics data

  • device type, operating system version
  • app identifiers
  • crash, performance, and feature usage data

2. Services and technologies we use

To operate the App, we may use third-party services:

2.1 Supabase

Supabase is used for:

  • user authentication
  • storing user data
  • account management

Supabase processes data in accordance with GDPR requirements and applies modern security measures.

2.2 Firebase (Google)

Firebase may be used for:

  • app usage analytics
  • error and crash tracking
  • improving stability and performance

Firebase may collect technical data, including device identifiers and information about interactions with the app.

2.3 Artificial intelligence (AI)

The App uses AI algorithms to:

  • generate meal recommendations
  • build menus and generate dish images

To generate meal plans, recipes, and personalized recommendations, the App may process user-provided inputs such as dietary preferences, restrictions, selected ingredients, and interaction data. Personal identifiers such as email address or real name are not intentionally sent to AI providers.

AI-generated content is provided for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical, nutritional, or professional advice.

For this purpose, user preferences and data entered in the App may be processed. AI processing is not used for automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

2.4 Advertising services (Ads)

The App may display ads.

Ads displayed in the App may be personalized or non-personalized, depending on the user’s location and consent status. For users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, ads are shown in compliance with GDPR requirements and applicable consent frameworks.

Users can manage or withdraw their consent for personalized advertising at any time through the app settings (if available) or via Google Ads settings: https://adssettings.google.com .

Advertising partners may:

  • use advertising identifiers
  • collect data about ad interactions

We do not share personal data such as name or email with advertisers.

3. How we use data

Collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • providing and supporting the app’s functionality
  • personalizing content and recommendations
  • improving service quality and usability
  • analytics and bug fixing
  • showing ads (if applicable)

We do not sell users’ personal data.

4. Sharing data with third parties

Data may be shared only with third parties necessary to operate the App, including:

  • Supabase
  • Google Firebase
  • advertising and analytics services

We use Supabase as our backend and database infrastructure provider. User data may be processed and stored on Supabase-managed servers under appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

We use Google Firebase for app usage analytics, error and crash tracking, and improving stability and performance. Firebase may collect technical data, including device identifiers and information about interactions with the app.

Data sharing is carried out in accordance with GDPR and applicable law.

5. Data storage and security

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or modification.

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Policy.

6. Account and data deletion

You have the right to delete your account at any time.

Deletion is available:

  • directly in the app, or
  • by contacting support

After account deletion, all associated personal data will be deleted or anonymized, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • obtain information about your data
  • request correction or deletion of data
  • restrict processing
  • withdraw consent to processing

To exercise these rights, you can contact us using the details below.

8. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal data.

9.International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed or stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Where required by law, appropriate safeguards are applied to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

We may update this Privacy Policy. The current version is always available on this page.
